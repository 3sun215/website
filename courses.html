Diploma in Computer Science

A Diploma in Computer Science is a short-term academic program that teaches the basics of computing, programming, software development, and IT systems. It is designed to equip students with practical and theoretical skills for careers in the tech industry or to continue with higher education in computer science.

Bachelor of Laws (LLB)

The Bachelor of Laws (LLB) is an undergraduate degree in law that provides the foundational knowledge and skills needed to pursue a career in the legal field.

Foundation in Business

The Foundation in Business is a pre-university program designed to prepare students for entry into undergraduate business degrees.
